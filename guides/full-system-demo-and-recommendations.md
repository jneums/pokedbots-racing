## üèÅ Race Strategy Guide

### Understanding Race Classes

**Scavenger Class (0-2 wins)**
- Entry Fee: 0.05-0.2 ICP
- Prize Pool: 5-20 ICP
- Competition: Beginner bots
- Strategy: Learn mechanics, build win record
- Recommended: All new racers start here

**Raider Class (3-9 wins)**
- Entry Fee: 0.2-1 ICP
- Prize Pool: 20-100 ICP
- Competition: Upgraded mid-tier bots
- Strategy: Specialized builds, terrain matching
- Recommended: After 3 wins and 1-2 upgrades

**Elite Class (10+ wins)**
- Entry Fee: 1-5 ICP
- Prize Pool: 100-500 ICP
- Competition: Highly upgraded premium bots
- Strategy: Min-maxed builds, perfect terrain/distance matching
- Recommended: Multiple upgrades, 65+ rating

**SilentKlan (10+ wins, invite only)**
- Entry Fee: 5-20 ICP
- Prize Pool: 500-2,000 ICP
- Competition: Best of the best
- Strategy: Elite GodClass/Master bots, full upgrades
- Recommended: Top 5% performers only

---

### Terrain Optimization

**MetalRoads (Most Common)**
- Favors: Speed + Acceleration
- Best Factions: EntertainmentBot, BattleBot
- Upgrade Focus: Velocity, Thruster
- Race Length: Usually MediumHaul (10-20km)

**WastelandSand (Rough Terrain)**
- Favors: Stability + Power
- Best Factions: WildBot, BattleBot
- Upgrade Focus: Gyro, PowerCore
- Race Length: Usually LongTrek (20-35km)

**ScrapHeaps (Technical)**
- Favors: Balanced stats, Acceleration
- Best Factions: Master, GodClass
- Upgrade Focus: Thruster, Gyro
- Race Length: Varies widely

---

### Distance Strategy

**ShortSprint (<5km)**
- Critical Stats: Speed, Acceleration
- Less Important: Power, Stability
- Best Bots: EntertainmentBot with Velocity upgrades
- Strategy: Fast start wins

**MediumHaul (10-20km)**
- Critical Stats: Balanced, slight Speed favor
- Less Important: None (all matter)
- Best Bots: BattleBot, balanced builds
- Strategy: Consistent performance

**LongTrek (20-35km)**
- Critical Stats: Power, Stability
- Less Important: Acceleration
- Best Bots: WildBot, BattleBot with PowerCore upgrades
- Strategy: Endurance over speed

**UltraMarathon (35km+)**
- Critical Stats: Power > Stability > Speed
- Less Important: Acceleration
- Best Bots: Upgraded Master/GodClass
- Strategy: Maximum Power upgrades

**After 3 Races:**

**After 10 Races:**

**Balanced Bot (all stats 55-65):**
1. Speed (biggest impact on race times)

1. Weakest stat first (bring to 60+)

### Per-Race Operating Costs

**Battery Depletion:**
- Each race: -10 battery
- Can race 5 times before recharge needed
- Recharge: 10 ICP (+10 battery, +20 condition)

**Condition Degradation:**
- Varies by race difficulty and results

## üí° Pro Tips & Advanced Strategies

### Market Timing
- **Monitor new listings:** Fresh bots often underpriced
- **Look for panic sales:** Players quitting list cheap
- **Avoid hype:** Master/GodClass sometimes overpriced
- **Buy between races:** Less competition when calendar empty

### Portfolio Strategy
**Own multiple specialized bots:**
- Sprint specialist (EntertainmentBot, high SPD/ACC)
- Endurance specialist (WildBot, high PWR/STB)
- All-rounder (BattleBot, balanced)
- **Benefit:** Enter optimal bot for each race type

### Reputation Building
- Win consistently in Scavenger to build record
- 3 wins ‚Üí Raider access (bigger prizes)
- 10 wins ‚Üí Elite/SilentKlan access (huge prizes)
- **Each win increases bot resale value**

### Upgrade Timing
- **Never upgrade during race events** (12-hour lockout)
- Plan upgrades for off-days
- Coordinate with recharge cooldowns
- Track upgrade completion time

### Selling Strategy
- Upgraded bots worth more (+10-20% per upgrade)
- Winning record adds value (+5% per win)
- Rare factions (Master, GodClass) hold value better
- List during high activity periods
